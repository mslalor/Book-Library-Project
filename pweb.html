Project Description:

The personal library book project is a website in which a person can log in, view, and manage their personal library.

They will be able to upload a book either by maunually entering it or scanning it in, edit the loaning status

(Rented, Available, Past Due), and send email reminders to students who have loaned their books.

There will be a ISBN database that has knowledge of the books and a database to store the information for each user.
